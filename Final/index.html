How to bake a pizza! Simple and Quick!

When the craving for Pizza strikes, your sure down to order a pizza for a few bucks from doordash! Well its easy to do it for free! Fire up your oven and follow these steps!

In the United States, Pizza is one of the most popular foodstuffs in the country, being quick delicious and easy to remember

  1. Step 1 Brush oil on the rims of the dough
  2. Step 2 Spin the dough for 3 minutes to maximize the size and texture.
  3. Step 3 Spread tomato sauce or sauce of your choice over the dough on a clean surface.
  4. Step 4 Put toppings of your choice on the dough, then bake at 375* for 30 to 40 minutes!
  5. Step 5 Chow down and enjoy!
